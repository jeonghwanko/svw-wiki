# Attribute System — Super Villain Wanted

## Five Attributes
- **Fire**: Strong vs Wood, Weak vs Water. Emotion: desire, passion (~28 characters)
- **Water**: Strong vs Fire, Weak vs Wood. Emotion: suppression (~23 characters)
- **Dark**: Strong vs Light. Emotion: isolation, grief (~19 characters)
- **Light**: Strong vs Dark. Emotion: perfectionism, masks (~17 characters)
- **Wood**: Strong vs Water, Weak vs Fire. Emotion: overprotection (~17 characters)

## Matchup
Fire → Wood → Water → Fire (triangle)
Dark ↔ Light (mutual advantage)

## Emotional Mapping (i-Girl Novel)
| Attribute | Emotional Pattern | Liberation Approach |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Fire | Unfulfilled desire | Acknowledge what they want |
| Water | Frozen feelings | Melt the ice, call their name |
| Dark | Self-imposed isolation | Reach through darkness with presence |
| Light | Performative masks | Show imperfection is okay |
| Wood | Overprotective love | Prove freedom is also love |
