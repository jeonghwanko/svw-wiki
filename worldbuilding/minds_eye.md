# Mind's Eye (심안, 心眼)

## Overview
- User: Iris (Ha-eun Lee)
- Type: Perception / empathy-based ability
- Function: Sees true emotions inside villains
- Cannot attack or defend — only see and connect

## Four Levels
| Level | Name | Korean | Ability | Unlocked |
|-------|------|--------|---------|----------|
| Lv.1 | Read | 심안 | See core emotion | Episode 1 |
| Lv.2 | Resonate | 심성 | Feel villain's emotions | Episode 5 |
| Lv.3 | Liberate | 심해 | Free suppressed emotions | Episode 10 |
| Lv.4 | Create | 심창 | Forge new bonds | Episode 13 |

## Liberation Sequence
1. Villain attacks → 2. Mind's Eye activates → 3. Inner world revealed
4. Connected to real-world truth → 5. Sincere words spoken → 6. Villain freed

## Weakness
Does not work on emotions Iris hasn't faced herself.
