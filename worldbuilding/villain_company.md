# Villain Company (빌런 컴퍼니)

## Overview
- Type: Organization
- Leader: The Captain (레드 다찌)
- Members: 97+ active villains
- HQ: Undisclosed location

## What is Villain Company?
The central organization in Super Villain Wanted. A corporate entity that recruits supervillains for roles ranging from world domination to BBQ grilling. Daily life resembles a dysfunctional but endearing office.

## Three Rules
1. Mission failure is not an option
2. Never betray a colleague
3. Lunch is every person for themselves

## Departments
- Operations, Marketing, R&D, General Affairs, Special Ops, HR, Facilities

## Hidden Truth (i-Girl Novel)
Behind the comedic facade, Villain Company harvests suppressed emotions. Each villain's power stems from unresolved emotional wounds. The protagonist Iris discovers that liberating villains through understanding is the key.
