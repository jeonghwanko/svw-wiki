# Episode 5: Trust, Not Orders — Season 1 Finale

## Info
- Season: 1 (Awakening) — FINALE
- Villain: Arkeon (아르케온) — SSS Fire
- Real Character: Jeong-hoon Park (박정훈) — Homeroom teacher
- Theme: Leadership
- Key Insight: "A real leader doesn't give orders from above. They say 'let's go together.'"

## Synopsis
Sports day disaster. The teacher has a perfect strategy but commands from the sideline without ever joining. The class finishes dead last. In the parallel world, the teacher is Arkeon — a mecha commander with absolute authority. Thousands obey, but no one stands beside him. Mind's Eye reveals his throne room: everyone salutes with their backs turned.

Ha-eun climbs the command tower and tells Arkeon to come down. "If you run with them, they'll run with you." His armor cracks. He descends. For the first time, his soldiers turn around.

Season 1 complete. Ha-eun has grown from silent observer to someone who reaches out. Mind's Eye evolves to Level 2.
