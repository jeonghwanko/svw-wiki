# Episode 2: How to Wake a Sleeping Star

## Info
- Season: 1 (Awakening)
- Villain: Stella (스텔라) — SS Water
- Real Character: Ha-rin Lee (이하린) — Younger sister
- Theme: Family
- Key Insight: "To wake someone who's sleeping, you have to call their name."

## Synopsis
Ha-eun's younger sister Ha-rin has locked herself in her room for a month. She won't answer knocks, won't eat, won't go to school. Ha-eun leaves toast at her door every morning, but never calls her name.

In the parallel world, Ha-rin exists as Stella — a Water villain who puts everything to sleep with a fog of lethargy. Through the Mind's Eye, Ha-eun sees Stella's inner world: a girl trapped in a bedroom, reaching for the door handle and pulling back, over and over. She wants to come out but is too afraid to open the door alone.

Ha-eun calls her sister's name: "Ha-rin. Come out. Your sister is right here." The fog lifts. The stars wake up. Back in reality, the door opens three centimeters. "...Unnie. Is the toast still there?"

## Key Moments
1. Ha-eun leaves uneaten toast at the locked door every morning
2. Two sisters sit back-to-back against the same closed door
3. Mind's Eye reveals Stella's inner world: a bedroom prison
4. Liberation through calling her name, not forcing the door
5. Return: The door opens three centimeters — a beginning
