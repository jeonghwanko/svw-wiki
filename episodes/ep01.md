# Episode 1: Mirror Beyond the Voice

## Info
- Season: 1 (Awakening)
- Villain: Mizuki (미즈키) — SS Fire
- Real Character: Seo-yoon Jeong (정서윤) — Best friend
- Theme: First Encounter
- Key Insight: "When you reach out with sincerity, walls come down."

## Synopsis
On her first day at a new school, introverted transfer student Ha-eun fails to speak to anyone. She discovers the most popular girl, Seo-yoon, secretly playing beautiful original piano music alone. Ha-eun wants to say "I liked your music" but can't.

That evening, Ha-eun enters a parallel world where Seo-yoon exists as Mizuki — a Fire villain who enslaves minds through enchanting music. Using her Mind's Eye, Ha-eun sees that Mizuki's true emotion is loneliness: she has never had a real audience.

The words Ha-eun couldn't say in reality become the key: "Your music was beautiful. And sad." Mizuki is liberated and becomes Ha-eun's first ally.

## Key Moments
1. Ha-eun hears Seo-yoon's secret piano in the music room
2. Mind's Eye awakens during Mizuki's enchantment attack
3. Ha-eun sees Mizuki's inner world: an empty concert hall
4. Liberation through sincere words, not combat
5. Return: Ha-eun texts Seo-yoon, friendship begins
