# Episode 4: Colors in a White World

## Info
- Season: 1 (Awakening)
- Villain: Bianca (비앙카) — SSS Light
- Real Character: So-yul Kim (김소율) — Class president
- Theme: Self-worth
- Key Insight: "Presence isn't given by others. It's made the moment you say 'I'm here.'"

## Synopsis
Ha-eun is invisible in class — forgotten in group projects, last picked for teams, her name not remembered. She tells herself it's comfortable. In the parallel world, Bianca erases all color and individuality from existence. Mind's Eye reveals Bianca lost her own identity and erases others to avoid being the only one who's different.

As Ha-eun's own color drains away, she realizes invisibility was never comfort — it was surrender. She reclaims herself: "I'm here. My color may be small, but it's mine." The white world cracks open to reveal a rainbow.

Back in reality, Ha-eun walks into her group project and, for the first time, volunteers her work before being asked.
