# Episode 3: A Monday Worse Than Death

## Info
- Season: 1 (Awakening)
- Villain: Thanatos (타나토스) — SS Dark
- Real Character: Min-hyuk Kang (강민혁) — Senior classmate
- Theme: Growing Pains / Burnout
- Key Insight: "Sometimes the person who carries everyone needs someone to say: you can rest."

## Synopsis
Min-hyuk Kang is the perfect student — president, volunteer, top 3 in school. Always smiling. But Ha-eun sees what no one else does: the dark circles, the lost weight, the whispered mantra on the emergency stairwell: "I can't stop. If I stop, it's over."

In the parallel world, Min-hyuk is Thanatos — the Grim Reaper who harvests not souls but time and willpower. His infinite grey corridor has no exit. Through the Mind's Eye, Ha-eun sees him walking endlessly, unable to stop because he believes stopping means everything collapses.

Collapsed under Thanatos's time-drain, Ha-eun manages: "You can rest. It's okay to stop. The corridor will still be there." Thanatos puts down his scythe. The grey walls crack open to sky.

Back in reality, Ha-eun gives Min-hyuk warm cocoa instead of energy drinks. "For actually resting." He smiles — not his usual perfect smile, but something real.

## Key Moments
1. Ha-eun sees Min-hyuk breaking down alone on the stairwell
2. Parallel world: infinite grey corridor with no exit
3. Thanatos harvests time/willpower, not souls
4. Mind's Eye: endless walking, the terror of standing still
5. Liberation: "You can rest" — the scythe drops
6. Return: warm cocoa replaces energy drinks
