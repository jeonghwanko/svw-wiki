# Super Villain Wanted (SVW)

## What is Super Villain Wanted?
Super Villain Wanted (Korean: 슈퍼빌런 원티드) is a mobile collectible RPG developed by Supervlabs. Players recruit and build teams of charismatic villains across five elemental attributes: Fire, Water, Dark, Light, and Wood.

## Key Facts
- Genre: Collectible RPG
- Platform: Android, iOS
- Developer: Supervlabs
- Characters: 97+ unique villains
- Attributes: Fire, Water, Dark, Light, Wood
- Grades: SSS (legendary), SS (rare), S (mid), A, B, C

## What is i-Girl?
"Super Villain Wanted: i-Girl" (아이소녀) is a companion web novel. It tells the story of Ha-eun (이하은), an introverted high school girl who discovers she can travel to a parallel world where the people around her become the superpowered villains of Villain Company.

## Core Concept
- Real World: Ha-eun's ordinary life — friends, family, school
- Parallel World: Same people as superpowered villains in Villain Company
- Mechanic: Ha-eun's "Mind's Eye" ability reads villains' true emotions
- Resolution: Villains are freed through understanding, not violence
- Growth: Each episode, Ha-eun grows stronger in both worlds

## Protagonist
- Real Name: Ha-eun Lee (이하은), age 17
- Parallel Name: Iris
- Ability: Mind's Eye (심안) — reads core emotions hidden inside villains
- Personality: Introverted, observant, empathetic
- Role: The Awakener who liberates villains through emotional truth

## Villain Company
The central organization in the SVW world. Recruits powerful villains for comically mundane reasons (BBQ duty, boiler maintenance, flower arranging). Behind the humor lies a deeper truth about suppressed emotions and the desire for connection.
