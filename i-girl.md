# Super Villain Wanted: i-Girl — Web Novel

## Synopsis
An introverted girl named Ha-eun discovers she can enter a parallel world where her classmates, family, and acquaintances exist as superpowered villains. Her unique ability — the Mind's Eye — lets her see the true emotions hidden inside each villain. Instead of fighting them, she liberates them by understanding their pain.

## Structure
Each episode follows a dual-world format:
1. **Reality**: A relatable conflict (school, family, friendship)
2. **Transition**: Emotional intensity triggers world-shift
3. **Parallel World**: Same conflict manifested as villain battle
4. **Resolution**: Emotional insight from reality defeats the villain
5. **Return**: Real-world relationship heals too

## Season Guide

### Season 1: Awakening (Episodes 1-5)
Ha-eun discovers the parallel world and learns to use Mind's Eye.
- EP01: First encounter with Mizuki — Theme: Connection
- EP02: Stella (sister) — Theme: Family
- EP03: Thanatos (senior) — Theme: Burnout
- EP04: Bianca (class president) — Theme: Self-worth
- EP05: Arkeon (teacher) — Theme: Leadership

### Season 2: Alliance (Episodes 6-10)
Ha-eun builds alliances and faces deeper emotional truths.
- EP06: Desmond (childhood friend) — Theme: Friendship
- EP07: Serena + Lena (idol + influencer) — Theme: Identity
- EP08: Ellara (club senior) — Theme: Truth
- EP09: Adriana (aunt) — Theme: Freedom
- EP10: Patra (grandfather) — Theme: Farewell

### Season 3: Confrontation (Episodes 11-13)
The final battle and Ha-eun's ultimate self-acceptance.
- EP11: Ryujin (rival) — Theme: Dreams
- EP12: All villains — Theme: Solidarity
- EP13: Ha-eun's shadow — Theme: Self-acceptance
